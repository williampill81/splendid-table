Our Story

Growing up a shy and sensitive introvert, I was fortunate to discover a Toastmasters International Gavel Club during COVID-19 distance learning. The Gavel Club expanded through the pandemic and my four years of high school. After 208 club meetings, I learned that an uplifting and constructive community transforms a person's perspective about themselves, and the world around them.

We provide an opportunity for Wildcats to learn about and appreciate each other. Be the change you want to see in the world.

——William Chang
